## 6. Method decision (important)

**Use motion tracking, not YOLO, for the ball on this footage.**
- Camera is fixed → 3-frame differencing isolates the fastest small object (the ball).
- Trajectory linking (net displacement ≥60 px, straightness ≥0.80, speed ≥6 px/frame) rejects player-limb jitter.
- No manual labeling required.
